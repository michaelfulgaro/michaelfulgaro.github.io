The Toony Hero

This was a controversial one. As far as the outfit goes, there was somewhat of a return-to-form to Link’s classic tunic, with a couple of additions and color changes. Link’s actual design as a character struck a nerve with many fans, as his proportions and art style were very cartoonish. Allegedly the next console Zelda game, originally set to feature this Link, had such a drastic change in art direction due to vocal fans.
