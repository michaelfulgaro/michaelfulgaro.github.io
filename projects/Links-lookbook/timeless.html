The Timeless Hero

This was the first major switch-up in Link’s appearance ever. Link was depicted as both a child and an adult during this era. Child Link did not wear the undershirt, leggings, or gauntlets. This particular iteration of Link was designed for The Legend of Zelda’s crossover to 3D on the Nintendo 64, and he was specifically designed to look “cooler” than ever before!
