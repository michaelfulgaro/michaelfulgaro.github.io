The Classic Hero

This was the very first guise that Link donned! It remained mostly untouched for several years. This outfit would later be revered as Link’s classic look as his appearance throughout the several installments in the series changed. When Nintendo began re-imagining the conventions of Zelda, they ironically began depicting him in this outfit once more.
